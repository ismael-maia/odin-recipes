Spaghetti Alla Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti alla carbonara is a classic Italian dish.

It consists of a combination of spaghetti, egg yolks, Parmesan cheese and bacon.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 100g Parmesan cheese
  • 150g bacon
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, cut the bacon into small pieces and cook it in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, mix the egg yolks with the Parmesan cheese and black pepper.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the bacon.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.
  6. Stir well until the sauce becomes creamy.
  7. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese and black pepper if desired.
