Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic dessert known for their soft texture and rich chocolate flavor. They are made with ingredients such as flour, butter, sugar, eggs, and chocolate chips.

These cookies are easy to make and are popular around the world as a sweet snack or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 150g butter
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 50g white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 150g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt to the mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Shape the dough into small balls and place them on a baking tray.
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Let the cookies cool before serving.
